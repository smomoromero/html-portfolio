The Best Kids Movies according to Sarah Momo

My Top 4 Kid Movies (most recent)

Coco

We watched this recently and it never fails to entertain.

Lyle, Lyle, Crocodile

Recommended by Shazi. The kids loved it, and the crocodile is voiced by Shawn Mendes, so great movie soundtrack as well!

The Nightmare Before Christmas

I will always love this movie for Halloween. I can't wait until the kiddos are old enough to watch this with me.

Inside Out

Both movies are great! They represent human emotions in such a fun and unique way.
